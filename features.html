Features of SakhiHealth

Comprehensive Health Chatbot

Our AI-powered chatbot assists with personalized health inquiries, providing guidance on women’s health topics and preventive care.

Period Tracker

A user-friendly period tracker enables women to monitor menstrual cycles and health patterns, with reminders and health insights.

Self-Assessment Quizzes

Interactive quizzes for health self-assessment help users identify potential health risks and take proactive steps toward well-being.

Appointment Booking

A seamless appointment booking system connects women with healthcare providers, supporting accessible and efficient healthcare access.

NGOs & Government Policies

Dedicated sections provide information on NGOs and policies focused on women’s health and rights, enhancing awareness and support.

Community Support Forum

A supportive forum for users to share experiences and advice, fostering a sense of community and mutual encouragement.
